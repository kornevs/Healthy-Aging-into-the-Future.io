Healthy Ageing

Maksims Kornevs
17 April, 2024

Welcome to this information material on healthy ageing. The material has been created within an EU-funded research project (POSITIVE) to disseminate knowledge about healthy ageing. The material was intended to be presented at two open physical seminars, but due to Covid-19 the material is available digitally on this page. The material is divided into five different short videos in five sections. Please scroll down the page to find them.

Finish watching one video and then move on to the next. After you have watched all the videos, please fill in a digital evaluation form to give us feedback on this material. The link to the evaluation form can be found after the last video.
