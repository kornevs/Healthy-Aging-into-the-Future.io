Nordic Congress of Gerontology

14 June, 2024

The 27th Nordic Congress of Gerontology featured a variety of presentation formats, including keynotes, symposia, oral presentations, and thematic poster tours. Additionally, spotlight sessions provided a platform for invited experts to explore key themes in an innovative and engaging way.

During the session titled "Future Well-Aging in a Transforming Digital World," the program's objectives and goals were presented. The presentation attracted significant interest from Congress participants.
